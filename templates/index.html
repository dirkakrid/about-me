Art of Software Engineer

I'm a Full Stack Software Engineer with interest in UX/UI Design from San Francisco. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, intuitive, minimalist interface designs. I attended San Jose State with a degree in Business Marketing. I worked at Groupon as a product operational specialist, launching multiple core products and taking those products from pilot stage to full mainstream launch. I developed standard operating procedures and testing protocols to ensure high product quality for end users. In 2013, I attended the Grace Hopper conference, where my interest in programming took root. Now I am a full time software engineer, who has passion for to code with design.

Design & Code

Wanderlust




Wanderlust allows world travelers to track their country or state visits using a D3 interactive map. This map enables users to click on a country or state. Once the country or state color has been changed, the visits are stored for future use. A user has the ability to upload a photo, which then becomes a postcard. The front displays a picture, while the back displays the address and description. Wanderlust also provides users with a timeline, in which they can add their photos and journal their travels. This app allows users to track all their travels in one convenient place. Discover new and exciting places and track all your world travels with wanderlust.
