Art of the Software Engineer

I'm a Full Stack Software Engineer from San Francisco with an interest in User Interface Design. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, intuitive, minimalist interface designs. I attended San Jose State with a degree in Business Marketing, and a Software Engineering certificate from Hackbright Acadamy. I worked at FATHOM I worked at as Full Stack software Engineer specializing in the development of high-quality user interfaces delivering optimal user experiences across web, application, and other platforms. Catalyst spurring collaborative communication among end users, designers, and developers while effectively creating well-received products. Extensive technical skills spanning Languages, frameworks & libraries, and applications & tools. Big picture thinker excelling in solution development for intricate problems. Prior to working at FATHOM I worked at Groupon as a product operational specialist, launching multiple core products and taking those products from pilot stage to full mainstream launch. I developed standard operating procedures and testing protocols to ensure high product quality for end users. In 2013, I attended the Grace Hopper conference, where my interest in programming took root. Now I am a full time software engineer with a passion for programming and design.

The Designer

I often feel people forget how creative software development can be. Each artist has her own medium. Some use canvas, some paper, some paint on walls. Wanderlust


Project Summary

Wanderlust allows world travelers to track their country or state visits using a D3 interactive map. This map enables users to click on a country or state. Once the country or state color has been changed, the visits are stored for future use. A user has the ability to upload a photo, which then becomes a postcard. The front displays a picture, while the back displays the address and description. Wanderlust also provides users with a timeline in which they can add their photos and journal their travels. This app allows users to track all their travels in one convenient place. Discover new and exciting places and track all your world travels with Wanderlust.

  • Stack:

    Pyton, JavaScript / jQuery, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3, D3.js
  • Client: Uma Petersen
  • Date: December 2015
  • Service: Web Development
